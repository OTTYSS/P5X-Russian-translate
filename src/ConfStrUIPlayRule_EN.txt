Based on your rank when a season ends	 you can obtain <color=#FF423F>season total rewards</color>.
These rewards will be sent to your in-game mailbox after calculation has completed.
Every hour	 you can obtain a portion of your <color=#FF423F>total rewards</color>	 based on your current rank.
Total rewards have a limit of 10	000 and must be obtained manually.
When you win a challenge	 you can obtain <color=#FF423F>victory rewards</color>.

When Phantom Thieves level up	 their stats increase.

A Phantom Thief's max level is limited based on the protagonist's level.

<color=#FFD145>Hidden Abilities</color>
To surpass the current maximum level	 a hidden ability must be enhanced. Whenever a hidden ability is enhanced	 you will obtain either new skills	 or level up skills you already have. Phantom Thief stats will also be enhanced at the same time.
Enhancing hidden abilities requires enhancement items such as Whiff of Iris or Fragrance of Iris. Hidden ability enhancement items can be obtained from Realm of Repression: Alluring Aroma.

<color=#FFD145>Awareness</color>
A Phantom Thief's awareness level indicates the degree of evolution of their Persona	 which can be enhanced up to level 6.
Increasing a Phantom Thief's awareness level raises awakening skill effects and greatly increases their strength in battle.
Increasing an awareness level requires a Phantom Thief's Cognitite.

123	<color=#FFD145>About Weapons</color>
Each Phantom Thief has certain weapon types they can use.
The higher the rarity and base ability of the weapon	 the stronger it can become.

<color=#FFD145>Enhancement</color>
When a weapon is enhanced	 its base ability is increased.
The enhancement level cannot exceed the current overclock level of the weapon.
Weapon enhancement level will be inherited when replacing weapons.
Enhancement items include metal parts	 silver parts	 and gold parts.
You can obtain these from clearing Realm of Repression: Pursuit of Parts.
Weapons that you no longer need can be used as enhancement materials.

<color=#FFD145>Overclock</color>
When overclocked	 a weapon's base ability can be greatly enhanced.
Weapon overclocking corresponds to Phantom Thief level.
Overclock level is transferred between equipped weapons.
Overclocking requires weapon overclock items.
Overclock items consist of Blue Crystals	 Purple Crystals	 and Yellow Crystals.
Overclock items can be obtained from Realm of Repression: Gear Forging.

<color=#FFD145>Forging</color>
You can forge a weapon to strengthen its effects.
Forge levels 1	 3	 and 5 require weapons of the same name	 while 2	 4	 and 6 require weapons of the same star level.

<color=#FFD145>Scrapping Weapons</color>
4★ weapons can be scrapped for 2 Gecko Coins if they aren't forged.
Forged 4★ weapons will grant extra Gecko Coins based on their forge level.
Note: 5★ weapons that can be forged with shards	 like Sennight Inferno	 cannot be scrapped.	About Weapons

Resist: Reduces damage dealt by 50%.
